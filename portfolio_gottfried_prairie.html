Rebranding

The Gottfried Prairie and Arboretum is a nature preservation and restoration project in Fond du Lac, Wisconsin. I wanted to give them new, more modern-feeling branding. I chose the word “connections” as my organizing concept, focusing on the connections between the plants, animals, and people that inhabit the space.

Working primarily in Photoshop, I designed a refresh for the Gottfried website. I wanted to make something attractive and energetic that surfaced all the information potential visitors might want right on a single page. (note: this is an embedded video; please click play if your browser doesn’t autoplay)

The logo for this project was a real challenge. I went through many iterations in Illustrator before settling on something that felt right for the brand and also functioned well in a variety of contexts.

In InDesign, I created a simplified brand spec sheet to guide further designs, such as merchandise, signage, posters, and additional website pages.
