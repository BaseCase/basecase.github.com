In an effort to get myself blogging more often, I've devised the following challenge: Learn as much as possible about one Unix command every week in 2009, and report on what I find.

I'm not limiting it to less common ones, either. Even absolute basics are up for consideration, since most of them have extra options that do cool stuff I don't know about yet.

My goal is to post a report each Sunday on what I learned that week, then pick next week's command. First week is 'screen'.

Feel free to drop suggestions for what to learn in the comments, and also share any nifty tidbits that I missed!
