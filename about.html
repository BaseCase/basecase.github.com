Hey

Hey there! I’m Casey. For a long time, I was a programmer. I mean, I still am one, but it was my job for quite a while. During that time, I coded up a lot of user interfaces and websites. Usually they were designed by someone else, and I worked off of a mockup in Figma or Photoshop. Every once in a while, I was the one “designing” the page.

It was always a big mystery to me what those designers were doing different from me. I could tell that things came out a lot better when they were involved, but I couldn’t articulate why or what was different. So a couple of years ago, I decided to fix that and went back to school.

Now, with a design degree under my belt, I’m starting to understand what makes the difference between an app or website that's well-designed and one that's designed by… well, by a programmer with no design chops. I know how software works under the hood, and I know how programmers think about it. And now, I know how to think about it from the other direction too: how to make things that users will find attractive, intuitive, and delightful.

Maybe you’re working on some software that needs a technically-minded designer to help connect it with users. If so, let’s chat about working together.
